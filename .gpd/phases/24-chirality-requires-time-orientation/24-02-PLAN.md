    **Step 2: State the third consequence.**
    (c) TIME-ORIENTATION: The chirality defined by omega_6 can only be physically realized (i.e., correlated with spacetime handedness to give the observed chiral fermion spectrum) on a time-oriented Lorentzian manifold. Therefore u creates a REQUIREMENT for time-orientation on the emergent spacetime.

    - (a) is CONSTRUCTIVE: u builds the gauge group via an explicit algebraic procedure.

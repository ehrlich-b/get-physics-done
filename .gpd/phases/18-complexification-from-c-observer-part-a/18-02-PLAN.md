    Extend the derivation from Plan 01 to track the full Jordan algebra complexification and the automorphism group upgrade.

    Step 9: Complexify h_3(O) to h_3^C(O).
    Define h_3^C(O) := h_3(O) tensor_R C. This is the complexified exceptional Jordan algebra.
    - Elements are 3x3 matrices over O_C = O tensor_R C (complexified octonions, dim_C = 8).
    - dim_C(h_3^C(O)) = 27 (same as dim_R(h_3(O)), now over C).
    - The Jordan product extends C-linearly: (a tensor z) * (b tensor w) = (a * b) tensor (zw).

    Note: h_3^C(O) is NOT the same as "3x3 Hermitian matrices over O_C" in the usual sense. It is the complexification of the real Jordan algebra. The "Hermiticity" condition extends: if X in h_3(O) satisfies X = X^dagger (octonion conjugation + transpose), then X tensor 1 in h_3^C(O) satisfies (X tensor 1)^# = X tensor 1 where # is the complex-linear extension of dagger.

    Step 10: Structure group upgrade F_4 -> E_6.
    - F_4 = Aut(h_3(O)): the automorphism group of h_3(O) as a Jordan algebra. dim(F_4) = 52.
    - Under complexification, F_4 embeds into E_6:
      - E_6 (compact form E_6^{-78}) is the structure group of h_3^C(O): the group preserving the cubic form (determinant) det(X) up to a phase.
      - More precisely: Str_0(h_3^C(O)) = E_6, the connected component of the structure group.
      - F_4 embeds as the subgroup of E_6 that fixes the real structure (i.e., preserves h_3(O) inside h_3^C(O)).
      - dim(E_6) = 78 = 52 + 26, where the 26 additional dimensions come from the complexification.

    State the chain explicitly:
    F_4 = Aut(h_3(O)) --[complexification]--> E_6 = Str_0(h_3^C(O))

    Step 11: Stabilizer upgrade Spin(9) -> Spin(10).
    - In F_4: Stab_{F_4}(E_11) = Spin(9), dim = 36.
    - In E_6: Stab_{E_6}(E_11) = Spin(10) x U(1), dim = 45 + 1 = 46.
      (The U(1) factor corresponds to phase rotations of the complex structure; in physics, it is identified with lepton number. We note this but do not analyze it in this phase.)
    - The upgrade Spin(9) -> Spin(10) is consistent with Plan 01: the stabilizer grows because the complexified Peirce space V_{1/2}^C = S_10^+ admits the larger Spin(10) symmetry.

    Track through the Peirce decomposition:
    - V_1 (1-dim) -> V_1^C (1-dim over C): unchanged, still the idempotent's eigenspace.
    - V_{1/2} (16-dim real) -> V_{1/2}^C (16-dim complex): Spin(9) -> Spin(10) as established in Plan 01.
    - V_0 (10-dim real) -> V_0^C (10-dim complex): the vector representation of Spin(9) extends to the vector representation of Spin(10).

    Cite Baez, Yokota, and Boyle 2020 for these identifications. Cross-check dimensions at each stage.
  </action>

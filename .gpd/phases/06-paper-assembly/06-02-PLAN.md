    Opening paragraph: "We now verify that the self-modeling sequential product defined in Section 3 satisfies all seven axioms of van de Wetering's Definition 2 [vdW2019]. By Theorem 1 of [vdW2019], this establishes that the state space carries the structure of a Euclidean Jordan algebra."

    **S1 (Additivity):** a . (b + c) = a . b + a . c. Follows from linearity of compressions C_{p_i} and Peirce projections P_{ij}. 2-3 lines.

    **S2 (Continuity):** a_n -> a implies a_n . b -> a . b. Follows from continuity of spectral decomposition in finite dimensions and continuity of sqrt on [0,1]. 2-3 lines.

    **S3 (Unitality):** 1 . a = a for all effects a. Proof: when a = 1, spectral decomposition is trivial (single eigenvalue 1), so 1 . b = C_1(b) = b. This is exactly where the corrected product succeeds and the naive extension failed (Section 3.2). 3-4 lines.

    **S4 (Orthogonality symmetry):** a . b = 0 implies b . a = 0. THIS IS THE KEY AXIOM. Detailed treatment:

    State the theorem formally. Two-case proof structure:
    - Case A (a full-rank): If all eigenvalues of a are positive, then a . b = 0 forces each C_{p_i}(b) = 0 (since lambda_i > 0 and P_{ij}(b) terms can be controlled by sqrt(lambda_i lambda_j) > 0). This forces b = 0, making b . a = 0 trivially.
    - Case B (a rank-deficient): Some eigenvalue lambda_k = 0. Then a . b = 0 and the spectral structure force b into face(p_k^perp) via Alfsen-Shultz facial absorption (Prop. 7.43): C_p(b) = 0 with b >= 0 implies b in face(p^perp). Once b lies in the complementary face, b . a = 0 by complementary-face orthogonality.

    State: "S4 is phi-independent -- it holds for any mixing function f with f(0,x) = 0, not just for the faithful choice f = sqrt." Note this means S4 is a structural property of OUS geometry, not dependent on the specific product.

    Reference Appendix A for full proof. ~15 lines in main text.

    **S5 (Compatible associativity):** If a and b are compatible (simultaneously diagonalizable), then a . (b . c) = (a . b) . c. Follows from functional calculus: compatible effects share a spectral decomposition, so compressions compose. 3-4 lines.

    **S6 (Compatible additivity):** If a . 1 = a (i.e., a is compatible with all effects), then for compatible a, b: (a + b) . c = a . c + b . c. Follows from a . 1 = a plus simultaneous diagonalizability. 2-3 lines.

    **S7 (Compatible multiplicativity):** For compatible a, b: a . b = a * b (the Jordan product). Follows from functional calculus commutativity on shared spectral decomposition. 2-3 lines.

    Closing paragraph: "With S1-S7 verified, Theorem 1 of [vdW2019] yields:"

    State as a Theorem environment: "Let V be a finite-dimensional spectral order unit space admitting a faithful self-model. Then V is order-isomorphic to a Euclidean Jordan algebra."

    Note: non-associativity of the product is consistent with vdW (associative => commutative by Westerbaan-Westerbaan-vdW 2020, and commutative => classical).

    Use \sp{a}{b} notation throughout. Use theorem/proof environments. Reference full proofs in Appendix A for S4. Use arXiv:1803.11139 for axiom statements.

    5.3 From Faithful Tracking to Local Tomography:
    - Define the correlation bilinear form: B(a,b) = tau(a * phi^{-1}(b)), where tau is the normalized EJA trace and * is the Jordan product.
    - Prove non-degeneracy: on a simple EJA, the trace form tau(a * c) is non-degenerate (Faraut-Koranyi III.4.2). Since phi is an isomorphism, phi^{-1} preserves non-degeneracy.
    - Conclude: non-degeneracy of B implies the product effects {a tensor b} are linearly independent in V_BM, spanning a dim(V_B) * dim(V_M) dimensional subspace.
    - By minimality (Assumption 3): dim(V_BM) = dim(V_B) * dim(V_M). This is local tomography.
    State Assumption 4 (simple EJA) here -- trace form non-degeneracy requires simplicity.

    5.4 Addressing the Entangled Sector: Brief discussion. The entangled sector consists of states in V_BM not reachable by product states. Minimality eliminates it by construction: V_BM is the MINIMAL OUS satisfying the composite axioms. For complex QM (M_n(C)), minimal = maximal composite, so this is consistent. For real or quaternionic QM, minimal != maximal, which is why those types are excluded.
